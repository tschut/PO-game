Phase 0: Initial Product Backlog prioritization

This is your inital Product Backlog. It contains all functionality your stakeholders requested. Every item has:
  • A description.
  • A business value. This is the expected value we will create by completing this item.
  • An estimate. This is the amount of work the team thinks it will cost to complete this item.
As Product Owner, it's your task to prioritize the Product Backlog. You can reorder items by drag and dropping them. Try to maximize delivered value by putting items with a high return on investment on top of the backlog!
